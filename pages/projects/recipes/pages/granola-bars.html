Banana pudding recipe

Description

This chewy granola ba recipe is extra delicious. You can make them your own by picking different ingredients to add! Try using any combination of miniature chocolate chips, sunflower seeds, raisins, chopped dried fruits, candy-coated chocolate pieces, chopped nuts, etc.

Ingredients

  • 4 ½ cups rolled oats
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • ⅔ cup butter, softened
  • ½ cup honey
  • ⅓ cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups miniature semisweet chocolate chips

Directions

  1. step

    Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13-inch pan.

  2. step

    Combine oats, flour, butter, honey, brown sugar, baking soda, and vanilla in a large bowl. Stir in chocolate chips until well combined. Lightly press oat mixture into the prepared pan.

  3. step

    Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, 18 to 22 minutes. Let cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then cut into bars. Cool completely in the pan before removing or serving bars.
