JPGs

JPG images are good for photosw with large palettes

GIFs

Good for simpe animations. Transparent pixels are a binary option for GIFs, meaning you can’t have semi-opaque pixels.

PNG

Good for diagrams and icons. For photos, a PNG file of the same quality would generally be bigger than an equivalent JPG file. However, they do deal with opacity just fine, and they don’t have color palette limitations.

SVG

SVG are AMAZING (for responsive design)Use them whenever you can.Unlike the pixel-based image formats above, SVG is a vector-based graphics format, meaning it can scale up or down to any dimension without loss of quality. pixels.

There is one potential issue with SVGs: for them to display consistently across browsers, you need to convert any text fields to outlines using your image editor (e.g., Abode Illustrator or Sketch).

Attribute in html

An HTML attribute gives additional information to an HTML element and always goes in the element’s opening tag. An attribute is usually made up of two parts: a name, and a value; however, not all attributes require a value. In our case, we need to add an href (hypertext reference) attribute to the opening anchor tag.
